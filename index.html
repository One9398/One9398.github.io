Question

Passing Data between View Controllers

how do I transfer the data from one view to another? I will be holding the selections on the UITableView in an array, but how do I then pass that back to the previous data entry form view so it can be saved along with the other data to core data on submission of the form?

Answer

  • Passing Data Forward by general

Controller直接暴露属性,在Controller之间切换时直接设置属性

  • Passing Data Forward using Segues

使用在UIStoryboard中的Segue进行控制器跳转时,在-(void)prepareForSegue:(UIStoryboardSegue *)segue sender:(id)sender方法中进行对目标控制器的数据传递

  • Passing Data Back by Delegate, Block, Notification
  • 使用NSUserDefaults
  • 使用单例对象
  • 数据库/文件访问

Question: Vertically align text to top within a UILabel

I have a UILabel with space for two lines of text. Sometimes, when the text is too short, this text is displayed in the vertical center of the label. How do I vertically align the text to always be at the top of the UILabel?

Answer: 使用UILable的sizeToFit进行尺寸自我适应

  • sizeToFit会根据label已设置的width进行细微调整
  • 如果文字过长需要换行,先设置numberOfLines为0.
  • 使用了autolayout的label在viedidload中调用了sizeToFit不会改变lable的尺寸位置,因为autolayout会在,进行自动布局前,撤销sizeToFit的调用影响;但是在viewDidLayoutSubviews中调用sizeToFit对label位置和尺寸有会影响.
