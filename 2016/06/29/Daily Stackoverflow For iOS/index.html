Question: Vertically align text to top within a UILabel

I have a UILabel with space for two lines of text. Sometimes, when the text is too short, this text is displayed in the vertical center of the label. How do I vertically align the text to always be at the top of the UILabel?

Answer: 使用UILable的sizeToFit进行尺寸自我适应

  • sizeToFit会根据label已设置的width进行细微调整
  • 如果文字过长需要换行,先设置numberOfLines为0.
  • 使用了autolayout的label在viedidload中调用了sizeToFit不会改变lable的尺寸位置,因为autolayout会在,进行自动布局前,撤销sizeToFit的调用影响;但是在viewDidLayoutSubviews中调用sizeToFit对label位置和尺寸有会影响.
